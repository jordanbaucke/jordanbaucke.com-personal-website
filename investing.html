Investing Philosophy

My interest in financial markets began early, shaped by family influences and experiences during the Global Financial Crisis. I've been personally involved in the stock and options markets, trading my own capital, for roughly 20 years. While I've read widely on investing and trading, I am not a professional money manager or trader. What the process has given me is a fairly honest understanding of myself—my strengths, and more importantly, my weaknesses when it comes to patience, risk tolerance, and conviction. Markets have a way of being very effective mirrors.
